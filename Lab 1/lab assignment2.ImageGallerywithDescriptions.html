Mountains are prominent landforms that have significant heights above sea level and/or the surrounding land. They are steeper than hills. A mountain or mountain range usually has a peak, which is a pointed top. Mountains have different climates than land at sea level and nearby flat land.



Tall Trees are an essential resource for everyone. They provide habitat for various species, clean the air and produce oxygen. Besides, they give us shade in the summer, and their leaves can be used for numerous purposes, such as making perfumes, medicines, etc. Moreover, they help cool our atmosphere.



Atm pin is a secret code that allows you to access your accounts and protects your personal information. It's similar to a password but made of numbers rather than letters. You need to enter a PIN when withdrawing cash from an ATM or making a transaction using a debit or credit card.



Koenigsegg Jesko Absolut is a high-performance hypercar produced by the Swedish manufacturer Koenigsegg. It is named after Jesko von Koenigsegg, the father of the company's founder, Christian von Koenigsegg. The Jesko Absolut is designed to be the fastest car in the world, with a top speed estimated to exceed 300 mph (482 km/h). It features a lightweight carbon fiber construction, advanced aerodynamics, and a powerful twin-turbocharged V8 engine that produces up to 1,600 horsepower when running on E85 fuel.
